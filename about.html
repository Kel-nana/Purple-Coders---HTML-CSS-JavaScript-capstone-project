PURPLE CODERS EVENT
2023

Purple Coders Event is ment to bring programmers from around the world with the common purposes of solving difficult brought forward by the audience at large. Build networks and bring a sense of brotherhood and belonging to the community. Promote the ideology of shared resources to the community and show the reward of this by tackling through some of the most complex concepts in the programming world. Promote individualisim as a means to creativity to be able to build new technologies and push the world just abit forward. This is what we aspire to as a community.
